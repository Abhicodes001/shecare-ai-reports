#  SheCare AI

SheCare AI is a mobile-first web application designed for the early detection of critical women’s health conditions using AI-driven analysis. The platform focuses on identifying risks related to PCOS, Endometriosis, Postpartum Depression, and Menopause-related complications through clinically inspired health questionnaires.

---

##  Features

-  User Registration & Health Profiling  
-  Clinically-Based Questionnaires (PHQ-9, EPDS, MRS inspired)  
-  AI-Powered Risk Prediction Engine  
-  Medical-Style Health Report Generation  
-  Mental & Behavioral Health Analysis  
-  Risk Visualization Dashboard  
-  Doctor Consultation & Appointment Request  
-  Shareable Health Reports  

---

##  Application Workflow

1. User Registration  
2. Health Data Collection via Questionnaires  
3. AI Risk Prediction  
4. Medical Report Generation  
5. Doctor Consultation  

---

## 🧠 Health Modules

### 1. Gynecological Screening (PCOS)
- Menstrual cycle tracking  
- Acne severity  
- Hair loss / excess hair  
- Weight gain & fatigue  

### 2. Endometriosis Detection
- Pelvic pain analysis  
- Menstrual pain severity  
- Digestive discomfort  
- Lower back pain  

### 3. Postpartum Mental Health (EPDS-based)
- Mood tracking  
- Anxiety detection  
- Sleep issues  
- Emotional well-being  

### 4. General Mental Health (PHQ-9 based)
- Depression indicators  
- Energy levels  
- Focus & interest tracking  

### 5. Menopause Monitoring (MRS-based)
- Hot flashes  
- Sleep disturbances  
- Mood swings  
- Physical discomfort  

### 6. Behavioral Health Indicators
- Sleep hours  
- Activity level  
- Stress levels  
- Screen usage
